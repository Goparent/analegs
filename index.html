Biography

A lawyer shaped by
two countries.

Ana Piernas López grew up in a Spanish family in Germany — a lived experience that became the foundation of a 20-year practice that no curriculum can replicate.

After completing two German State Law Exams and qualifying at the Munich Bar, Ana relocated to Madrid in 2001, building a dual practice that operates simultaneously within both legal systems. Today, Piernas Abogados advises individuals, families, and businesses on the most complex cross-border matters between Spain and Germany.

Ana's expertise extends beyond courtrooms: she is a MiKK-trained transboundary family mediator (Berlin, 2013), a certified Parenting Coordinator (ANCOPA, 2019), and co-founder of Plataforma Familia y Derecho, an initiative to establish specialized family courts across Spain.

She teaches International Family Law and Mediation in master's programs across Spain, and has published in leading legal reviews on cross-border family matters.
